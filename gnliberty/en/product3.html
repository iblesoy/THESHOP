Fire Rated Glass

Specific fire protection equipment, glass for fire protection equipment. This is an excellent fire-resistant glass that can withstand
both fire and water simultaneously. It contributes to a bright and comfortable space while also providing safety.
Most frequency of occurrence, fire out of disaster and Fire protection glass has an important role to minimize damage caused by fire.

Fire Lite, which is born from super heat-resistant glass-ceramics that is strong enough to withstand rapid heating to 800℃ and spraying
cold water due to thermal expansion coefficient is almost zero, is the only specific fire protection equipment and glass for fire protection
equipment that is not destroyed by rapid temperature rises in the event of a fire or rapid cooling by water spraying for fire suppression.
Fire Lite, a glass for specific fire protection devices, has been proven to guarantee customer safety as it meets UL certification standards
in the United States. Unlike wired glass, it provides a clear view and has the same transparency as float glass.
- Field : Housing & Construction, Kitchen & Home Appliances. Business & Commercial Facilities, Airports & Subway Stations, etc.
- Functions / Characteristics : zero thermal expansion coefficient, super heat-resistance, high designability, safety.

Features
