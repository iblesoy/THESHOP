NIPPON ELECTRIC GLASS Co.,Ltd

NEG (Nippon Electric Glass Co., Ltd.) was founded in 1944 and independent from NEC in 1949.
Starting with vacuum tube glass and tube glass, entered the TV CRT glass business in 1964 and
has been developed and grown. After that, it began producing crystallized glass, architectural glass,
electronic component glass, and glass fiber, and began producing LCD substrate glass in the 1990s.
In the 2000s, in response to changes such as the rapid growth of the Flat Panel Displays market and the
sharp decline in the existing CRT market, it actively transformed its business structure, and is currently
recognized worldwide for its excellence as one of the world's top three glass manufacturers for
Flat Panel Displays. In the Top Plate Glass sector, NEG has supplied more than 90% of the Japanese
domestic market to Panasonic, Hitachi, Mitsubishi, Sharp, and Harman, and recently has been developing
and supplying Top Plate Glass with excellent texture and the ability to implement various colors.
In the future, we will do our best to help create added value and enhance the competitiveness of
domestic electric ranges and IH range products.
