Neoceram N-0, N-11

Super heat-resistant glass-ceramics <Neoceram>

Super heat-resistant glass-ceramics <Neoceram>is used widely due to its excellent thermal shock resistance,
mechanical strength, hardness, electrical properties, etc.
It is provided in the shape of plates, tubes, press products, etc. according to the molding and processing method.
Stoves, heating windows, etc. are representative applications. It is also used in the electronics and information fields,
such as carrier for furnace of electronic components due to its characteristic of having a thermal expansion coefficient close to 0.

Neoceram is a super heat-resistant glass-ceramics which is thermal expansion coefficient close to zero.

There are two types of Neoceram : transparent N-0 and opaque N-11.
Special Features
- Excellent thermal shock resistance
- Excellent heat resistance, zero porosity
- High-precision finished surface, 8.5G size is available for carrier glass purpose.

Neoceram N-0

N-0 Neoceram is a transparent glass-ceramics which is thermal expansion coefficient almost close to zero.
It can be used in continuous thermal cycles at temperatures below 800℃.
N-0 plate glass is used for color filter substrates and micro lens array substrates of polysilicon TFT liquid crystal displays.
- Field: LCD, telecommunication terminal devices, LTPS / AMOLED
- Function / Characteristics: Low coefficient of thermal expansion, heat resistance.
- Shape: Plate
- Application : heater cover glass for LTPS / AMOLED fields and setters for transporting thin substrate glass,
heating appliances such as stoves, high-temperature heat treatment equipment, etc.

Neoceram N-11

N-11 Neoreram is white ceramic glass which is low thermal expansion coefficient.
It can be used in continuous thermal cycles at temperatures below 800℃.
It is used for the atmosphere temperature and particles prevention in the furnace.
- Field : Muffle glass in the LTPS/AMOLED field and setter for substrate glass carrier, etc.
